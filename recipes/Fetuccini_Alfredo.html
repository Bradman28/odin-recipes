Fetuccini Alfredo

Description

This pasta has been one of my favorites since I was a child. Super easy to make, and super fun to eat!

You can pull this dish together in about 15 minutes, so give it a try!

Ingredients

  • Fetuccini Noodles
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Heavy Cream (or half and half)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Parsley
  1. Heat salted water to a boil. Add noodles
  2. Combine butter and garlic in sauce pan until garlic is slightly browned
  3. Pour in half and half and heat until lightly simmering
  4. Add Parmesan Cheese
  5. Once all ingredients in sauce are combined, add in noodles. If desired, add pasta water to thin sauce
  6. Top with fresh parsley (optional)

You can add chicken, shrimp, or even steak if you would like a protein in it. Also goes well with broccoli, mushrooms, onions, or leeks.
