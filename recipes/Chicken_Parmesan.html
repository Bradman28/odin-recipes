Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is one of my favorite comfort foods. It is great along side pasta, and fun to make homemade marinara as well!

I have found that this recipe works best with all fresh ingredients. Don't be afraid to buy higher quality mozzarella and herbs, as well as marinara from scratch.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Fresh Basil
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  1. Filet and pound out chicken until about 1/2in thick, and season lightly with salt and pepper
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  3. Put flour, egg, and Panko breadcrumbs in 3 separate dishes
  4. Season flour generously with salt and pepper. Add grated Parmesan cheese to Panko breadcrums
  5. Coat chicken pieces with flour, then egg, then panko. Set aside until all are coated
  6. Heat pan with just enough olive oil until bottom is coated and oil starts to shimmer
  7. Carefully place chicken in pan, flipping after 3 - 4 minutes until browned
  8. Once all pieces are browned, place in a baking dish
  9. Top with marinara, fresh mozzarella, and fresh basil. Cook in oven until cheese is melted and slight browned
  10. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes

You can serve this dish with a side of pasta if desired. Enjoy!
